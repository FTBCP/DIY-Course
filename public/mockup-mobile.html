Understanding Amortization Schedules
5 min read · Video included · 3 sources

What is Amortization?

Amortization is the process of spreading loan payments over time. Each payment covers both interest and principal — but the ratio shifts dramatically over the life of the loan.

In the early years, the vast majority of your payment goes toward interest. As the loan matures, more goes toward paying down the actual balance.

Why This Matters

Most borrowers are surprised to learn how little principal they've paid down after years of on-time payments. This is especially pronounced with 30-year mortgages.
